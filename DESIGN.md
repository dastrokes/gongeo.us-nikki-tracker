# gongeo.us Design System (Luminous Whimsy)

## Visual Identity

**gongeo.us** is a resonance tracker and community hub for _Infinity Nikki_. The brand aesthetic is whimsical, magical, and soft, drawing inspiration from high-fidelity game art and ethereal fantasy landscapes.

## Design Tokens

### Colors

- **Action Pink:** `#FF6B8B` (Primary brand color for buttons and CTAs)
- **Soft Rose:** `#FFF0F3` (Soft background and secondary surfaces)
- **Deep Slate:** `#333333` (Primary text color for high contrast and readability)
- **Surface:** `#fff8f7`
- **Glass White:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.8)` (Used for translucent containers)

### Typography

- **Primary Font:** `Outfit`
- **Headings:** Outfit (Semi-Bold)
- **Body:** Outfit (Regular)

### Layout & Style

- **Border Radius:** 16px (Standard), 999px (Pills/Buttons)
- **Shadows:** `0 4px 20px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.05)` (Soft, subtle depth)
- **Visual Effects:** Glassmorphism, soft glows, and whimsical transitions.

## Components

- **Top Navigation:** Translucent glassmorphic bar with rounded pill-style links.
- **Buttons:** Highly rounded corners (pill-shaped) with soft hover states.
- **Cards:** White surfaces with subtle shadows and 16px rounded corners.
